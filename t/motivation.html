no motivation.

it doesn't matter what you're doing, burnouts are inevitable. maybe you're coding, drawing, writing, whatever. any creative task is bound to make you tired and eventually run out of ideas.

you can't avoid it, sadly. but you can helf yourself out of it. here's how i do it:

  1. relax. take a break, close your device and have a breath of fresh air.
  2. find inspiration from other works, and when you feel like it, mess around.
  3. when you're done messing around, you can start doing serious things again!

this works out for me every. single. time, and it's bound to work for you, too. it's extremely simple and yet people somehow still don't understand it.

tl;dr: being demotivated or burnt out is ok, take a break and start fresh.
